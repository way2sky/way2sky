Projects

Blowing Blues (AKA Breeze) explores the health applications of interactive media using an interaction mechanic based on breath sensing and control. Winner of the top software demo award at ESCoNS 2013 and has been presented at MeaningfulPlay and Games for Health. USC Interactive Media MFA Thesis Project : Blowing Blues.


HertzianRide visualizes artificial magnetic field intensity during an Amtrak train trip from Oceanside station to LA Union station, creating a layer of meaning that augments our natural senses.


Hertzian Explorer is a data visualization of electromagnetic waves of wireless communication. Using novel integration of existing technologies, the installation allows viewers to experience the effects of cell phone use. Shown at DorkBot SoCal in July 2009.


TorrentWatch is an interactive visualization application of worldwide Bittorrent movie exchange traffic. Users can see high-level patterns and by rotating around a "waterfall" of chronological data, analyze specific details of the torrent traffic. Was featured on Software Studies Initiative culturevis.com.


ReVis is a J2ME based mobile phone application that visualizes the RSS feed from infosthetics.com in a reflexive visualization of the site's latest entries.


Patriot Performer: a visual performance instrument in which users can play and manipulate visuals in real time that relate to the concept of patriotism.


LogoPainter reveals CountryWide's (now Bank of America) stock fluctuations. The size of the pointillist dots used to render the CountryWide logo varies depending on stock price, creating shifting moments of clarity and chaos.


SyneTyper is a visual performance application designed for the grapheme color synesthete. The performer types letters to generate the associated hues. Synesthetes can communicate via this program by parsing the visuals and non-synesthetes can experience a taste of being synesthetic.


Solar Talking Plant: When "watered" with a flashlight, the electronic creature comes to life by producing a diverse and changing range of sounds--the plant's secret language.


DragonFly: A 3D educational computer game integrating basic physics education into gameplay.


BubbleTimes performs code generated visual and rhythmic animation of stock market volatility and economic bubbles.


Out of Control: A short film that reflects on the saying "a spoiled rose will not grow" by depicting the rebellious nature that results from excessive parenting.


Softimage renderings some using high dynamic range(HDRI) lighting techniques. (2005)


Patent for a customizable watch consisting of modular band pieces that can be combined together, allowing the user to remix features for different needs. (2004)
