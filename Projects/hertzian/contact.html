xuan.li@way2sky.com
www.way2sky.com

Xuan Li creates works that merge concepts and ideas from different disciplines
into new digital and electronic expressions. He has worked in the areas of web
design, game level design, product design, and 3D rendering and animation. His
recent works attempt to expand the role of information visualization as an art form
through a novel combination of physical sensors with software based algorithmic
visuals, exploring new aesthetic possibilities by expressing the nature of wireless
data flow.
